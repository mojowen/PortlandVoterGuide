Coming Soon!

We are still collecting information on the Porltand Mayoral candidates - our current list of questions include:

  • Do you support Portland becoming a Sanctuary City so that all immigrants can live without fear of being separated from their families?
  • Do you support gender equity for city boards?
  • Do you support the City of Portland creating a new office to regulate air pollution?
  • As Mayor would you require city agencies to regularly conduct equity audits looking at hiring and promotion of city staff and contractors to ensure equal pay and increased diversity across and within departments?
  • Do you support public funding for campaign finance?
  • Do you support legal and safe spaces to socially consume cannabis?
  • Do you support repealing the 48 hour rule, which allows police to not answer questions about an incident for 48 hours after using force?
  • Do you support immediate freeze on rent prices in Portland?
  • Do you support allowing homeless families to live in temporary tent communities?
  • Do you believe in using congestion fees to improve commutes and public transit?

Suggestions are more than welcome - get in touch

Ballot Measures

Measure 26-174

What it does

Five year levy: Oregon Historical Society Library, Museum, educational programs

A yes Vote Means...

Renew the levy, which remains at $.05 per $1,000 assessed property value (a home assessed at $200,000 continues to pay $10 a year).

Who says yes
  • Yes for Oregon History
endorse

A no Vote Means...

The levy is not a renewed.

endorse

Measure 26-173

What it does

Temporary Motor Vehicle Fuel Tax for Street Repair, Traffic Safety.

A yes Vote Means...

Adopts a four year, 10 cents per gallon fuel tax dedicated to street repair, safety (including safer crossings, sidewalks)?

Who says yes
  • Fix Our Streets Portland
  • City Club of Portland
endorse

A no Vote Means...

The fuel tax is not adopted.

Who says no
  • Lila Leathers
  • Taxpayer Association of Oregon
  • Paul Romain
